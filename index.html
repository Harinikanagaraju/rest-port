Our Story

Authentic Flavours
from the Heart

Born from a deep love of Malaysian and Singaporean street food culture, Take Away brings the authentic tastes of Kuala Lumpur and Singapore's hawker stalls right to the heart of Madurai. Every dish carries generations of tradition — bold spices, true heritage, made with love.

From fragrant Nasi Goereng to layered Parottas and rich Indian gravies, our menu is a culinary journey across two continents — crafted for the bold, the curious, and those who believe food is culture.
